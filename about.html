About Us

Initiating You

Initiating You, as the name suggests is a platform for the young people to initiate their professional lives, where our team seeks to provide them with overall development by the means of career counselling, CV building and some great internship opportunities, which include professional, virtual and hobby based internships. In simpler words, we lend you the edge you need over your peers for success.
Why choose us? Well, now this is a simple question to answer. We provide you with best opportunities and services, which no one else can. We work with passion and that is the only thing that has brought us here, where we stand today. Give us a chance to serve you, and we will redefine the term quality for you!

Our Team

We aren't huge in head count, just a small group of young enthusiasts constantly looking for a way out of the rat race. Initiating You has been started by four individuals, tired of the monotony of life, who wanted to spark a change in the opportunities available to people today.
